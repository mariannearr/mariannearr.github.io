I develop non-autoregressive foundation modeling paradigms and architectures for flexible and efficient sequence generation beyond fixed left-to-right order. My PhD research introduced new diffusion language models for parallel and flexible-order text generation, with first-author publications at ICML, NeurIPS, and ICLR. Notably, I led the development of Block Diffusion, featured as an oral at ICLR 2025, which is adopted by dLLMs at Google, NVIDIA, Meta, ByteDance, and others.

I am interning at NVIDIA Research with Pavlo Molchanov and have previously interned at Runway AI with Anastasis Germanidis. My CV is available here.

I am on the job market starting Summer 2027. Please reach out if you know of research roles in generative modeling that may be a good fit.
